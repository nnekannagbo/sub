10-month rooftop romance
  1. Return
  2. February 26, 2022 at 7:55 AM

Men in suits on the brown rooftop of a building, some time in the morning, so it appears. The reflection of the eastward sun shines a golden ripple across the surface of the rooftop. The rooftop has windows. 15 square-shaped, dark, tinted windows with a white trim.

The men spread out across the rooftop. Talking in pairs. Talking in threes. And far in the back there’s one man, not wearing a suit, standing by himself. His arms folded, his right leg crossed in front of his left leg. He wears a scarf and baseball cap. And he’s looking right at you.

Toward the bottom left of the rooftop, in the shade, there’s a woman off to the side. She is in the vicinity of a conversation involving two men, but she is not a part of it.

In the background there are more buildings. Grey buildings. Brown buildings. Black buildings. Their windows reflect the blue sky and white clouds of the day.
